2027 vs itself · Excel R3

  • Level monotonicity — L1 ≥ L2 ≥ … ≥ L5 on 17,860 rows

  • No duplicate item+size rows
  • Raw ↔ ArtRoom — summarized prices match source varieties
  • CK ArtRoom ↔ COMBOKITDATA — 1,200 rows identical
  • Size ladder — 26→51→144→288→512 (256 = 512 cut in half late in cycle, prices near 512)

  • CAD/USD ratio — Zone A / Zone 2 ≈ 1.343 ± 0.01
  • Zone ladder — every US break is 2→3 (3,123 items); CA A→E has 0 breaks
  • Multi-flag items — 20 SKUs fail ≥2 independent tests (Helianthus, steep-zone class, Erysimum)
