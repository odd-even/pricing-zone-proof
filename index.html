RESOLVED Zones C·D·E freight boxes — not published, same as 2026

The 2026 catalogs for C, D and E also had no box-shipment rates on the Shipping Information page (no Box Shipments section, zero dollar amounts) — the omission is the established design for those zones, not a missed fill. The ws 2027 sheet does list C/D/E rates ($72/$37/$18, $79/$33/$22, $120/$90/$50) if they’re ever wanted in the catalog.

ZONES 5·6 Rack minimums — PDF matches 2026, ws 2027 sheet disagrees

Zone 5 PDF says 2 racks and Zone 6 says 3 racks — identical to the 2026 catalogs. The ws 2027 freight sheet says the opposite (3 for Zone 5, 2 for Zone 6). So either the sheet has them swapped, or the minimums really changed and the PDFs are carrying stale 2026 text. Ask which source is right; box charges match the sheet everywhere.

RESOLVED Combo accessories = 2026 exactly, including the $69.98 Bowl

Every accessory price in every zone is identical to the 2026 catalogs — "repeat last year's pricing" was executed correctly. The 12" Bowl's flat $69.98 across zones 4/5/6 was in the 2026 books too. Full table in the Combo accessories section below.

RESOLVED 256-tray pricing near 512 rates — intentional

A 256 is a 512 tray cut in half manually near the end of its production cycle — same small plugs, half the count — so it prices just above 512 per-plant rates. The 2026 files labeled this size "Half-512". Same basis both years (Ageratum):

2026   512 = $0.103  ·  Half-512 = $0.120
2027   512 = $0.103  ·  256 = $0.127

ZONE 3 Cheaper than Zone 2 — new this year

96.5% of items (and all 120 combos) are ~0.8% cheaper in Zone 3 than Zone 2. In 2026 this was not the case: Zone 3 averaged +0.5% above Zone 2 with zero cheaper items. The flip is new in 2027 — confirm the zone rate table is intentional.

ALL ZONES EOD math changed vs 2026 — wording now overstates it

2027 builds regular price as EOD × 1.1, which makes the discount 9.09% off regular. 2026 was a true 10% (EOD = regular × 0.9, exact across 5,675 rows). The numbers are self-consistent, but the Discounts page still says "billed at 10% off the regular price" — that was literally true last year and isn't now.

RESOLVED Discounts-page sample table — static by design

The 2026 catalogs did the same thing: one example item (Ageratum Aloha Blue, Zone E-level numbers) identical in all 10 PDFs. The sample is a static illustration of how to read the table, not live zone data. 2027 continues the pattern with Ageratum Kona.
